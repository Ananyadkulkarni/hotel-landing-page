ADK HOTEL



SERVICES PROVIDED

The ADK Hotel offers a wide range of services to cater to the needs of its guests.

Accommodation: The hotel offers a variety of luxurious rooms and suites, including options with city views and private balconies.
Dining: Guests can enjoy a variety of dining options, including gourmet restaurants serving international cuisine, a poolside bar, and room service.
Spa and Wellness: The hotel's spa offers a range of pampering treatments, such as massages, facials, and body wraps.
Fitness Center: Guests can stay active at the hotel's well-equipped fitness center.
Swimming Pool: The hotel features a sparkling outdoor swimming pool.
Concierge Services: The hotel's concierge staff can assist guests with arranging tours, transportation, and other services.
Business Center: The hotel offers a business center with meeting rooms and office equipment.
Wi-Fi: Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
Valet Parking: Valet parking is available for guests.
Room Service: Guests can enjoy meals and drinks delivered directly to their rooms.
This list is not exhaustive, and the hotel may offer additional services.
